# AI & Design Daily Digest — 2026-06-24

## Executive Summary

### Executive Summary: AI & Design Developments (June 24, 2026)

*   **OT Cybersecurity Breakthrough:** Dragos launched **EmberAI**, an operational technology-native platform that leverages historical industrial data to automate incident response and prioritize security threats based on direct operational impact.
*   **Persistent Agentic Collaboration:** Anthropic introduced **"Claude Tag,"** a new Slack integration that functions as a persistent team member, capable of accumulating institutional knowledge and executing tasks asynchronously without continuous human prompting.
*   **Enterprise Agent Orchestration:** Verint unveiled its **"Agent Factory,"** a specialized environment designed to blend human and AI agents, alongside new intelligence tools to automate and measure complex contact center workflows.
*   **Financial & Technical Integration:** Dukascopy Bank integrated **MCP (Model Context Protocol) servers** to allow AI assistants (Claude/ChatGPT) to execute trades and manage risk directly within JForex, while Claude Code received updates for improved CLI-based workflow and background agent management.
*   **Streamlined AI Content Creation:** Instruqt expanded its AI enablement suite by launching a track-building plugin for Claude Code and adding native support for **Google Vertex AI and Amazon Bedrock**, significantly accelerating the production of hands-on technical training content.
