# AI & Design Daily Digest — 2026-07-02

## Executive Summary

### Executive Summary: AI & Design Research (July 2, 2026)

*   **Scientific Visualization & Predictive Modeling:** Anthropic launched **Claude Science**, a specialized workbench that natively renders complex 3D genomic and chemical structures, while Ai2 released **MolmoMotion**, an open-weight model capable of predicting precise 3D object movement in video based on natural language inputs.
*   **Enterprise Agent Governance:** To mitigate security risks, **Nuggets** introduced cryptographic access controls for LangGraph, and **Netzilo** expanded its runtime behavioral monitoring to cover CrewAI, LangGraph, and Microsoft Foundry, providing essential oversight for autonomous enterprise agents.
*   **Infrastructure & Workflow Reliability:** Google’s **ADK 2.0** now integrates deterministic "Workflows" to improve LLM-based agent reliability, supported by **Couchbase’s new AI Data Plane**, which provides a framework-agnostic persistence layer to standardize data handling for LangGraph and CrewAI agents.
*   **Model Expansion & Global Access:** Google broadened its ecosystem with the **Gemini Omni Flash** and **Nano Banana 2 Lite** models, while Anthropic successfully restored global access to **Claude Fable 5**, now reinforced with an updated cybersecurity classifier following the removal of US export restrictions.
