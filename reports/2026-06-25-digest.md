# AI & Design Daily Digest — 2026-06-25

## Executive Summary

### Executive Summary: AI & Design Innovations (June 25, 2026)

*   **Figma Config 2026 Breakthroughs:** Figma unveiled a suite of production-focused tools, headlined by "Code Layers" for one-click design-to-code conversion and "Figma Motion," an open-beta tool enabling direct keyframe animation within the canvas.
*   **Next-Generation Design Agents:** Figma’s upgraded AI agent now supports Model Context Protocol (MCP) connectors, custom skill management, and web search grounding, significantly enhancing design-to-development automation and external tool interoperability.
*   **Gemini 3.5 Flash "Computer Use":** Google launched a public preview of "Computer Use" for Gemini 3.5 Flash, allowing the model to autonomously interact with desktop, mobile, and browser environments via simplified action intents, bridging the gap between LLMs and UI control.
*   **Specialized AI in Healthcare & Business:** The Children's Hospital of Philadelphia introduced "RareDAI," an LLM-based diagnostic assistant that leverages chain-of-thought reasoning to improve genetic test selection; concurrently, Jotform launched an AI App Builder to automate business application deployment.
*   **Agentic Orchestration:** Adobe and Accenture Song debuted an "Agentic Experience Orchestration" framework, designed to synchronize customer experience (CX) platforms with external agent ecosystems via MCP, signaling a shift toward enterprise-wide autonomous workflow integration.
