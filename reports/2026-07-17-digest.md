### Executive Summary: AI & Design Research (July 17, 2026)

*   **Moonshot AI Unveils Kimi K3:** The new 2.8 trillion-parameter, natively multimodal model features a 1-million-token context window; Moonshot AI has committed to an open-weights release by July 27, 2026.
