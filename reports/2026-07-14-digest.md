# AI & Design Daily Digest — 2026-07-14

## Executive Summary

### Executive Summary: AI & Design Research (July 14, 2026)

*   **Advancements in AI Reasoning & Simulation:** Skyfall AI launched the **MORPHEUS** platform, a specialized environment for continual reinforcement learning, while ByteDance introduced **UniVR-34B-Planning**, a 34-billion parameter model explicitly optimized for high-level reasoning and planning tasks.
*   **Enhanced Design-to-Code Capabilities:** Anthropic has upgraded **Claude Code** with a sandboxed in-app browser, enabling the AI to directly interact with live websites and reverse-engineer UI components via a new "select element" feature, significantly streamlining design-to-development workflows.
*   **Direct AI-to-Production Workflows:** New Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers from **Tiiny Host** and **Markifact** now allow users to publish live websites and manage Google Ads campaigns directly within Claude and ChatGPT interfaces, removing the need for local infrastructure or manual dashboard navigation.
*   **Enterprise Agent Security & Scalability:** Addressing the rise of autonomous systems, **Okta** unveiled an identity-centric security framework that treats AI agents as managed identities with granular entitlements and "kill-switch" controls, while **Digital.Marketing** expanded its platform to enable agencies to deploy white-labeled, multi-step agentic workflows at scale.
