# AI & Design Daily Digest — 2026-06-11

## Executive Summary

Here's an executive summary of the most important AI & Design research findings from today (2026-06-11):

*   **Google AI Achieves Significant Speedup in Text Generation with DiffusionGemma:** Google AI has released DiffusionGemma, an experimental open-source model that leverages text diffusion techniques to achieve up to 4x faster text generation on dedicated GPUs compared to traditional autoregressive methods.
*   **Gemma 4 12B Enables On-Device Multimodal AI:** Google DeepMind's Gemma 4 12B, an encoder-free multimodal model, now allows for the processing of text, images, audio, and video in a single pass, running efficiently on standard 16GB laptops.
*   **Wenge AI Introduces Decitron for Advanced Decision-Making:** Wenge AI has launched Decitron, a new general decision-making foundation model, following its successful HKEX listing hearing, indicating advancements in AI for complex scenario analysis.
*   **Financial Stability Board Proposes AI Risk Management Framework:** The Financial Stability Board (FSB) has released a consultation on 12 sound practices aimed at mitigating AI risks within the financial sector, prioritizing financial stability in its recommendations.
